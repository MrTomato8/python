About

We, a group of python coaches, from Berlin would like to spread the joy of programming with Python. So we decided to give introduction workshops as well as on-going python beginners workshops on special topics on a regular bases. At the moment we are in progress of creating the material for the beginners course as well as the follow up workshops. But we'll present some more details here shortly.

Introduction Afternoon

But if you are interested in learning programming, you probably want to mark 1st of September 2012 in your calendars already. Because on that afternoon we'll be hosting our first Introduction to programming with PythonTurtle.

We'll organise the event using the OTS meetup group, so you might wanna sign up for that already, too.

Schedule

This introduction workshop will consist of two parts: the friday evening and the saturday afternoon.

Friday evening

We organise a little gettogether and install party. If you don't have Python 2.7 on your laptop just yet, bring it by and we install it together. It is not mandatory to come to this meeting but it also gives you the chance to get to the coaches as well as other participants in relaxed and casual environment upfront. Well, and to get the requirements done ;) .

Satuday afternoon

On Saturday afternoon we gather together to get our hands dirty. We'll be meeting at 2pm and, start with a quick introduction by one of the coaches, who's also taking you through what we want to do that afternoon and then head right into hacking our first little scripts and programs using the awesome Turtle in python.

About OpenTechSchool

OpenTechSchool is a community initiative to provide programming and other tech-related workshops to women and their friends. We take care of the organizational side of things (venues, press, finding participants) and let our volunteer coaches to create the curriculum according to their vision. Our main goal is to create a friendly and safe learning environment where no one feels shy about asking any question. Everyone, no matter what their gender, skill level or experience, is invited to participate, whether as a coach or a learner. OTS is an idea we would be very happy to see spread across the globe!

If you want to know more about OTS, check out our Website, our Blog and most importantly; join our mailinglist.
